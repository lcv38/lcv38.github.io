My School

I am majoring in Innovation, Technology, and Design at the University of Miami. The University of Miami is a private research university located in Coral Gables, Florida. It was founded in 1925 and has since grown to become one of the top universities in the country. The campus is situated on 239 acres and features a mix of modern and traditional architecture. The university offers over 180 undergraduate and graduate degree programs across 12 schools and colleges. The student body is diverse, with students from all 50 states and over 150 countries. The university is known for its strong programs in business, engineering, marine science, and music. It also has a vibrant campus life, with over 300 student organizations and a strong athletic program. Overall, the University of Miami is a prestigious institution that provides students with a high-quality education and a supportive community.

Inovation, Technology, and Design is a multidisciplinary major that combines elements of business, technology, and design thinking to prepare students for careers in innovation and entrepreneurship. Its offered through the University of Miami's School of Engineering, and it focuses on developing skills in areas such as product design, user experience, digital marketing, and business strategy.
