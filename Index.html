Who Am I?

My name is Lucas Vianna.

I am a student at the University of Miami, my major is Innovation, Technology ,and Design. I've always had a passion for inventing whatever it may be. I work on cars, boats, and anything one can think of. My passions are the water, building and restoring cars, and adventure. I was born in Miami, spent 9 years in Indiana and have been back here since. My entire family is Brazilian and I am always traveling back to visit loved ones. I have traveled all around, done everything from bungee jumping in New Zealand to going into an deactivated volcano in Iceland. I've always loved pushing limits, going after objectives and staying true to myself.
